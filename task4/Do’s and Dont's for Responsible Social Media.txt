# Do’s for Responsible Social Media Use:
1.Think before you post – Make sure your content is respectful and appropriate.
2.Protect your privacy – Adjust privacy settings and avoid sharing personal information like your address or phone number.
3.Verify information before sharing – Check facts to avoid spreading misinformation.
4.Maintain a positive digital footprint – Share content that reflects your values and goals.
5.Respect others online – Be kind, avoid cyberbullying, and engage in healthy discussions.


# Don’ts for Responsible Social Media Use
1.Don’t overshare personal details – It can put your safety at risk.
2.Don’t post in anger or frustration – Emotional posts can harm relationships and reputation.
3.Don’t engage in online arguments or trolling – It can escalate conflicts and reflect poorly on you.
4.Don’t plagiarize or misuse content – Always give credit to original creators.
5.Don’t ignore digital boundaries – Respect others’ privacy; don’t post photos or information without consent.
